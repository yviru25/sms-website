About Us

Mobicom SL Ltd is a premier mVAS company that provides authentic local and international content to the Sierra Leonean telecom consumers.
With a large partnership with preprofessional and experienced content creators, Mobicom provides premium SMS Alert, Bulk SMS, IVR, Digital Music and Videos.
Our modern and easy to use platform allows quick operator integration, content partner registration and clients to make bulk SMS purchase remotely.
Mobicom also provides business advisory and digital marketing services to help Sierra Leonean business connect and engage with their target customers easily.
Our army of certified digital and social media marketing professionals, business development specialists, brand development experts, tech and innovation enthusiasts have several years of professional experience working with all kinds and scale of businesses from around the globe.
As a niche mobile solutions company, Mobicom focuses on creating unique high impact solutions rather than run-of-the-mill value added services or strategies which offer no differentiation.
